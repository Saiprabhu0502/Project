History of IPL

The Indian Premier League (IPL) is a professional Twenty20 cricket league in India. It was founded by the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) in 2007. Since then, it has become one of the most popular and lucrative cricket leagues in the world.

Teams and Players

IPL features several teams representing different cities in India. Some of the prominent teams include Mumbai Indians, Chennai Super Kings, Kolkata Knight Riders, and Royal Challengers Bangalore. These teams have some of the best cricketers from around the world.
